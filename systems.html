Control4

The Control4 platform gives you easy control over every aspect of your home, whether or not you are present.

Entertainment

With Control4, distributed entertainment is a breeze. Multiple audio and video zones allow for each member of the household to view or listen to entertainment on their own terms.

Lighting

Control4 allows for easy integration of your home's existing lighting to provide endless automation options. Preset scenes can help set a variety of modes, whether it be a low-light romantic dinner or a well-lit social gathering.

Comfort and Convenience

Want to change the temperature of your hot tub? Or perhaps the afternoon glare is particularly strong through a window? Control4 has you covered. With a range of integrated devices, including your pool, hot-tub, blinds/shades, and thermostat, all it takes is the push of a button.

Security

Security systems provide you with peace of mind by ensuring your valuables and loved ones are protected, and the authorities alerted, in the event of unexpected guests. Control4 easily integrates with your current home security system, giving you quick and easy access to the system's status, warnings, and keypad. Arm or disarm your security system from the same device that controls the rest of your home.

Customizable to your needs

Control4 can be custom built to your needs! Much of the existing devices, appliances, and technology in your home can be incorporated into your system as you see fit. Contact Infinity Automation today for more information about custom system integrations.
