Lights

Lighting control, preset lighting scenes, and timed lighting events are all hallmarks of an integrated home. Zigbee wireless technology allows for the quick automation of pre-existing lighting in your home; all it takes is a new switch. Integration with Control4 allows for total control of your lights regardless of where you are. Preset scenes allow you to control multiple lighting zones with the push of one button. Is the sun about to set? One push will dim the lights to 50%, setting the mood for the coming evening. Heading to bed? One button can turn off all your home's lights, while simultaneously leaving a temporarily-lit path to your bedroom.
