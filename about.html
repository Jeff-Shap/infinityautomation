Who is Infinity Automation?

The team at Infinity Automation comprises of a small band of local and knowledgeable employees. Learn a little more about each of us below, then give us a call to meet us in person!
Josh
Born in South Dakota but raised in Sarasota, Josh has always had a passion for new technology. Combining his previous expertise in consumer electronics with his strong desire to operate a business, Josh created Infinity Automation in February of 2016 and hasn't looked back. He acts in mulitple capacities as the owner, operator, and a field technician.
Fernando
Hailing from Boston originally, and Miami more recently, Fernando brings years of invaluable technical experience to the field on Infinity Automation's behalf every day. An avid family man, Fernando devotes most of his free time to his wife and children.
Barbara
Born and raised in Chicago, Barbara recently closed her 24-year-old business in residential cleaning to "man the helm" that is the Infinity Automation office. She handles day-to-day operations as well as all coorespondance and scheduling. As our primary point-of-contact, you can almost always count on her voice on the other end of the line, should you ever need to call us.
Jeff
Born and raised in Sarasota, Jeff is the newest addition to the Infinity Automation family. With a background in computers and home security, Jeff is quickly learning the ropes of the home automation industry and is excited to continue his career with us.
